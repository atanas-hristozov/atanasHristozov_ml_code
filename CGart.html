CG Art

In the computer generated art every detail is important.I always use models the highest quality, the best textures and various softwares in order to deliver the perfect rendering. During my study in SOA academy and Redvertex academy, I mastered composition, ligthning, modeling and adding the propoer mood to the images.

Human eye level

In humman eye levels, the composition is everything. I always keep the ligntnig and the shadows in mind when i set-up the mood. The precise coloring is vital,especially were I need to stress the importance of an item.

Interior

In interior, attention to detail is crucial. Therefor I always use textures with high-end quality, detailed models and model the geometry in high detail. Lightning is of key importance, so I use it in it's max potential.

Aerial view

There is no better way to introduce a vision for a city or a landmark building than an areal render. When I chreate those images, I select the best urban models, use the best-quality textures and generate the topology of the terrain using the most up to date softwares.

3d floor plans

There is no better way for the clients to see their architectural 2D drowing in 3D floor plans. When I generate those, I always include detailed 3D models, use hi-end textures and cut the plan in the most aye-catching way.

Concept Art

There is nothing better than the joy of creation. With computer graphics, I can express my visions in a stunning way. I can freeform and texturize my dreams and establish an unique form of art.
