who am i

My name is am Atanas and I am an architect and a CG artist. I have always had passion for arts as well as for mathematics. This is why I decided to study architecture as it combines both of my strengths. I graduated with Master degree in Architecture at the UNIVERSITY OF ARCHITECTURE,CIVIL ENGINEERING AND GEODESY (UACEG) in Sofia, Bulgaria. During my studies, I discovered how to digitally create images and animations and fit them in the best way possible in my project presentations. After graduation, I joined the REDVERTEX academy, which helped me boost my skills in architectural visualization. At the academy I managed to advance my skills under the supervision from industry leading artists.

Soon after I finished REDVERTEX's academy course, I joined CAST studio - one of the leading architectural studios in Bulgaria. Where I gathered practical experience and invaluable skills both as an architect and as a CG artist.

I wanted to further improve my visualization skills and decided to join STATE OF ART (SoA) academy in Venice, Italy. There I met leading artists in the field, who taught me different approaches of editing images and animations in the final stage when the finishing touches are applied. I had the opportunity to expand my knowledge in the newest software products. There I got my certification for industry leading software products
