Hangzhou trade centre

Hangzhou Trade centre is a sustainable urban hub for trading, working and leisure located in Hangzhou, one of China’s most picturesque cities. It is a part of the economical city development in China. Situated 180 kilometres south-west of Shanghai, Hangzhou is one of China’s most prosperous cities, especially renowned for its scenic landscapes. Located in Qianjiang New Town near the Qiantang River, this edifice becomes a major landmark along the green axis of the city’s new CBD. A rich mix of 24/7 functions occupies almost 400,000m2 within two streamlined towers set atop a podium and landscaped plaza.

The formal romanian embassy

Situated in the downtown of Plovdiv, Bulgaria, this building underwent a lot events of throughout 20th century. Originally build as a Romanian embassy in the 19th century, this edifice survived two Balkan Wars, two World Wars and the Cold War.

Many of the architectural details were unable to stand the test of the time, but with a massive research in 2020 this project was made to preserve this building and restore/refresh to its original beauty.

Interior in Philipopolis

A fresh minimalistic interior design with a sense of the magical atmosphere of mid-20th century. By using natural materials and appropriate color palette, despite the small volume of this apartment, it leaves you with a “hygge”, comfortable feeling. The main feature of this project is the nice balcony overlooking the city where you can catch a glimpse of the old town brewery’s chimney.
